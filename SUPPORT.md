# Support

Need help with Lofa? Pick the right channel:

## For shoppers

- Web: <https://lofa.tn>
- Email: **hello@lofa.tn**
- Instagram DM: [@lofa.tn](https://instagram.com/lofa.tn)

## For seller brands

- Become a seller: <https://lofa.tn/auth/register?role=SELLER>
- Brand partnerships: **brands@lofa.tn**

## For developers

- **Bug?** Open a [Bug report](./.github/ISSUE_TEMPLATE/bug.yml).
- **Idea?** Open a [Feature request](./.github/ISSUE_TEMPLATE/feature.yml).
- **Security issue?** See [SECURITY.md](./SECURITY.md). Do **not** open a public issue.
- **Code question?** See [CONTRIBUTING.md](./CONTRIBUTING.md) or email **dev@lofa.tn**.

## Status & uptime

- API health: <https://api.lofa.tn/health>

We aim to acknowledge all messages within 2 business days.
